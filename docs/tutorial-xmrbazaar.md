# Tutorial: Purchasing XMR via XMRBazaar.com

> Version: March 2026
> Strategy: Peer-to-Peer (P2P) trading with 2-of-3 Multisig Escrow.

---

## 1. Introduction
XMRBazaar is a decentralized P2P marketplace for the Monero community. It functions without a central authority, allowing users to trade fiat, goods, or other cryptos for XMR. In 2026, it is the primary No-KYC alternative to centralized exchanges.

## 2. Setting Up
1.  **Navigate:** Visit [xmrbazaar.com](https://xmrbazaar.com).
2.  **Anonymous Account:** You do not need an email. Choose a username and a strong password. 
    *   **Warning:** There is no "Password Reset" feature. If you lose your password, you lose your account and reputation.
3.  **Prepare Wallet:** Have a non-custodial wallet (e.g., Cake Wallet or Monero GUI) ready to receive your XMR.

## 3. Finding a Seller
1.  **Browse Listings:** Go to the "Cash to XMR" or "Exchange" category.
2.  **Reputation Check:** Prioritize sellers with:
    *   **High Trade Count:** (e.g., 50+ successful trades).
    *   **Positive Feedback:** Review recent comments from other buyers.
    *   **Trust Rating:** Check the seller's aggregate rating on the platform.
3.  **Payment Method:** Select an offer that supports your method (e.g., Cash by Mail, Zelle, SEPA, BTC).

## 4. Executing the Trade (Multisig Escrow)
Never trade with a new seller without escrow.

1.  **Start Chat:** Use XMRBazaar's encrypted chat to contact the seller.
2.  **Request Escrow:** Ask the seller to use **2-of-3 Multisig Escrow**.
    *   **Participants:** You, the Seller, and a trusted **Mediator** (chosen from the site's approved list).
3.  **Fund Escrow:** The seller deposits XMR into the multisig address. Verify the deposit on the platform before sending any money.
4.  **Send Payment:** Send your fiat or crypto to the seller via the agreed method.
5.  **Mark as Paid:** Click the "Mark as Paid" button in the trade interface.
6.  **Release:** Once the seller verifies your payment, they release the funds to your Monero address. If a dispute arises, the Mediator will step in to review evidence.

## 5. Security & Safety
*   **Start Small:** If using a new seller, do a small "test" trade first.
*   **Check URLs:** Always ensure you are on the official `xmrbazaar.com` domain to avoid phishing.
*   **Encrypted Communication:** Keep all trade-related talk on the platform's chat so the Mediator can see it if there is a dispute.
